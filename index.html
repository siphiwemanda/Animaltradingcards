Kea

Kea's have been nick-named "The clown of the mountains"

  • Scientific Name: Nestor Notabills
  • Average Height: 48cm
  • Habitat: New Zealand
  • Conservation Status: Endangered

The kea is one of 10 parrot species in New Zealand. Its a mostly olive green parrot. With a grey beak, and orange feathers under its wings. Kea's live in groups of about 13 and have an average lifespan of about 5 years.
